Void Chat

Private and Highly Secure Communication

Fully anonymous mobile application that provides end-to-end encryption of your communication that is stored only on your device. Absolutely no personal information required. The only way to reach you via our chat is your own willingness. Supports iOS and Android platforms.

Geo Office

Staff Attendance Management

Mobile application that allows check-in and discovery in the virtual office that covers user defined geographical area. Real life usage example: movie production area often changes its location while director should be able to keep track of people present.

Software for Secure Communication

  • No one bothers you with the friend requests
